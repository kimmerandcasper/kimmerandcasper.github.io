About Us

Kimmer and Casper, LLC specializes in comprehensive fire damage restoration services. Our experienced team is dedicated to restoring homes and properties affected by fire, smoke, and soot damage. We understand the emotional and financial impact a fire can have, and we are here to guide you through every step of the restoration process, from initial assessment to complete reconstruction.

Our Services

We provide a wide range of services including debris removal, structural repairs, smoke and soot cleaning, and interior reconstruction. Our goal is to restore your property to its pre-loss condition as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Why Choose Us?

At Kimmer and Casper, LLC, we take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to quality. Our team uses the latest tools and techniques to ensure the highest standard of restoration work. We work closely with homeowners to provide personalized solutions that meet their specific needs.

Our Process

From the moment we arrive at the scene, we conduct a thorough inspection to assess the damage and develop a detailed restoration plan. We keep you informed throughout the process, ensuring transparency and peace of mind. Our team handles everything from emergency board-up services to final touches.
