Our Comprehensive Services

At Kimmer and Casper, LLC, we provide a full spectrum of fire damage restoration services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Our comprehensive services include:

  • Damage Assessment: Our experts conduct a thorough evaluation of the fire damage, creating a detailed restoration plan.
  • Debris Removal: We ensure safe and efficient removal of debris, preparing the site for restoration work.
  • Structural Repair: We rebuild and reinforce fire-damaged structures, restoring their integrity and safety.
  • Smoke and Soot Cleaning: Our team provides complete cleaning to eliminate all traces of smoke, soot, and odor from the property.
  • Interior Reconstruction: From drywall installation to painting, we handle every aspect of interior restoration to bring your home back to life.
  • Insurance Coordination: We work closely with your insurance company to facilitate a smooth claims process, ensuring you receive the coverage you deserve.
  • Final Inspection: After the restoration is complete, we conduct a final inspection to ensure every detail meets our high standards and your expectations.
