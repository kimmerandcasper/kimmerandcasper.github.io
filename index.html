Our Comprehensive Services

At Kimmer and Casper, LLC, we offer a full suite of fire damage restoration services, tailored to meet the specific needs of each client. Our services include:

  • Damage Assessment: Our experienced professionals conduct a thorough evaluation of the fire damage to develop a detailed restoration plan.
  • Debris Removal: Safe and efficient removal of debris, ensuring the site is prepared for restoration work.
  • Structural Repair: We rebuild and reinforce fire-damaged structures to restore integrity and safety.
  • Smoke and Soot Cleaning: Comprehensive cleaning to remove all traces of smoke, soot, and odor from the property.
  • Interior Reconstruction: From drywall installation to painting, we handle all aspects of interior restoration.
