The Great Adventure
My service dog, Casper, and I are going to go on a great adventure around the United States of America; a spiritual journey during which I plan to study the power of prayer for WordFlock.com and find meaning in my life after battling cancer twice. I plan on selling my most valuable possessions, leaving from Richland, and begging for gas from station to station as I pray with people and document the affect that prayer has on my life and others.
